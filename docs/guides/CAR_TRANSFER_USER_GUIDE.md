# Car Ownership Transfer Request - User Guide

## Overview

The Car Ownership Transfer Request system allows you to request ownership of a car
currently registered to another member of the Lotus Elan Registry. This is commonly
needed when you've purchased a car from another registry member, or when there's been a
change in ownership that needs to be reflected in the registry.

## When to Use Transfer Requests

**Transfer requests** are only for cars that already exist in the registry under another owner's name.
If a car does not exist previously in the registry, please go to the section
**[How to Add Your Car](/docs/guide-viewer.php?doc=ADD_CAR_GUIDE.md)**.

Use the transfer request system when:

- ✅ You've purchased a car from another registry member
- ✅ A car ownership has changed but the registry still shows a previous owner
- ✅ You need to claim ownership of a car with a duplicate chassis number entry

## Prerequisites

Before requesting a transfer, ensure you have:

- ✅ **Active registry account** - You must be registered as a user in the Lotus Elan Registry and be logged in
- ✅ **Contact information** - Your account email address for notifications must be up-to-date
- ✅ **Accurate car information** - You must have chassis number, year, model details, even if the record hasn't
- ✅ **Proof of ownership** - Be prepared to provide documentation if requested

## Step-by-Step Transfer Request Process

### Step 1: Find the Car in the Registry

1. **Search for the car** using the registry search function
2. **Locate the specific car** you want to transfer
3. **Verify the details** match your car (chassis number, year, model)

### Step 2: Access the Transfer Request Form

1. **Navigate to the car's details page**
2. **Look for the "Request Ownership Transfer" button**
   - Usually located in the car information section
   - May be labeled with a transfer icon (🔄)
3. **Click the transfer request button**

### Step 3: Complete the Transfer Request Form

Fill out the required information:

**Car Details (Pre-filled from existing record):**

- Chassis Number
- Year
- Model/Series
- Current registry information

**Your Information:**

- Your contact details (automatically populated from your account)
- Additional comments explaining the transfer reason

**Required Fields:**

- ✅ **Chassis Number** - Must match exactly
- ✅ **Year** - Vehicle year
- ✅ **Model** - Elan model and variant
- 📝 **Comments** - Explanation for the transfer request (recommended)

### Step 4: Submit Your Request

1. **Review all information** carefully
2. **Add any relevant comments** explaining why you're requesting the transfer
3. **Click "Submit Transfer Request"**
4. **Confirm submission** when prompted

### Step 5: Confirmation and Next Steps

After submission:

- ✅ You'll receive a **confirmation email** with your request details
- 📧 The **current owner will be notified** via email
- 👥 **Registry administrators** will be notified for oversight

## What Happens After You Submit

### Notification Process

1. **Current Owner Notification**
   - Receives email with your transfer request
   - Includes your contact information (first name and email)
   - Contains car details and your comments
   - Provides approval/denial options

2. **Administrator Notification**
   - Registry administrators receive notification
   - Oversight for transfer process
   - Available for assistance if needed

### Current Owner Decision Process

The current owner has three options:

1. **✅ Approve Transfer**
   - Ownership immediately transfers to you
   - You receive confirmation email
   - Car appears in your registry account

2. **❌ Deny Transfer**
   - Transfer request is declined
   - You receive notification with reason (if provided)
   - You can contact administrators if you disagree

3. **⏳ No Response**
   - Request remains pending
   - Follow up options available
   - Administrator intervention may be needed

## Timeline Expectations

**Typical Transfer Timeline:**

- **Immediate:** Confirmation emails sent
- **1-7 days:** Current owner response time (varies)
- **Same day:** Transfer completion (if approved)

**Note:** Some transfers may take longer depending on owner responsiveness and any complications that arise.

## Tracking Your Request Status

### Email Notifications

You'll receive emails for:

- ✅ **Request confirmation** - When you submit
- 📋 **Status updates** - If there are changes
- ✅ **Approval notification** - If transfer is approved
- ❌ **Denial notification** - If transfer is denied (with reason)

### Checking Status

Currently, request status tracking is handled via email notifications. If you need to check the status of a pending request, contact registry administrators.

## After Transfer Approval

When your transfer is approved:

1. **Ownership Update**
   - Car ownership immediately transfers to your account
   - Car appears in your registry profile
   - Previous owner loses access to the car record

2. **Registry Access**
   - You can now edit car information
   - Upload photos and documentation
   - Update car details as needed

3. **Confirmation**
   - Receive confirmation email with new ownership details
   - Previous owner receives notification of completed transfer

## Common Issues and Troubleshooting

### Transfer Request Not Showing

**Problem:** Can't find transfer request button

**Solutions:**

- Ensure you're logged in to your account
- Verify the car isn't already under your ownership
- Check that the car exists in the registry
- Contact administrators if the button should be there but isn't

### Current Owner Not Responding

**Problem:** No response to transfer request after reasonable time

**Solutions:**

- Wait at least 7-14 days for response
- Check if the current owner's email is valid/current
- Contact registry administrators for assistance
- Administrators can help facilitate communication

### Transfer Denied Unexpectedly

**Problem:** Transfer denied and you believe it should be approved

**Solutions:**

- Review the denial reason provided
- Contact the current owner directly if appropriate
- Provide additional documentation to administrators
- Request administrator review of the decision

### Duplicate Chassis Numbers

**Problem:** Multiple cars with same chassis number

**Solutions:**

- Transfer requests help resolve these situations
- Provide clear documentation of your ownership
- Work with administrators to determine correct ownership
- May require additional verification steps

## Tips for Successful Transfers

### Before Requesting

- ✅ **Verify ownership** - Ensure you have legitimate claim to the car
- ✅ **Gather documentation** - Bills of sale, titles, etc.
- ✅ **Check contact info** - Ensure your email is current
- ✅ **Research the car** - Verify details match your vehicle

### Writing Effective Comments

- 📝 **Be specific** - Explain the circumstances of the ownership change
- 📝 **Be polite** - Courteous tone helps with approval
- 📝 **Provide context** - When/how ownership changed
- 📝 **Include timeline** - When the ownership change occurred

### Working with Current Owners

- 🤝 **Be respectful** - They're being asked to give up registry access
- 🤝 **Provide information** - Help them understand the legitimate transfer
- 🤝 **Be patient** - Allow reasonable time for response
- 🤝 **Communicate directly** - If appropriate and contact info is available

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I request transfer of any car in the registry?

**A:** No, you should only request transfers for cars you legitimately own or have purchased.

### Q: What if the current owner doesn't respond?

**A:** After a reasonable waiting period (7-14 days), contact administrators for assistance.

### Q: Can I cancel a transfer request?

**A:** Contact administrators to cancel pending requests if circumstances change.

### Q: What information does the current owner see about me?

**A:** They see your first name, email address, and location, plus any comments you included.

### Q: How long do transfer requests stay active?

**A:** Requests remain pending until approved, denied, or cancelled by administrators.

### Q: Can I request multiple transfers at once?

**A:** Yes, but each car requires a separate transfer request.

### Q: What if my transfer is denied?

**A:** You can contact administrators to review the decision, especially if you have documentation
supporting your ownership claim.
