# Lotus Elan Identification Guide

This is not a comprehensive list of differences between the cars, rather
a quick guide to identifying a car. Like all manufacturers, there were
running changes throughout the life of the Elan. In the case of Lotus,
many of these changes were poorly documented. If in doubt, post a
question to the [LotusElan.net forum](http://www.lotuselan.net/forums/)
and ask.

## Roadster/Drophead

### Elan 1500 (*Type 26 Elan 1500 Roadster*)

- 1500 badge on boot
- All were recalled from the factory and updated to Elan 1600 specification
- Boot lid does not extend all the way to the trailing edge of the car
- Lift up windows

### Elan 1600 (*Type 26 S1 Roadster*)

![Type 26 Elan 1600 (S1) showing round tail lights and lift-up windows](faq/screenshots/identify/elan1600.jpg)

- 1600 badge on boot
- Round tail lights
- No rollup windows
- Boot lid does not extend all the way to the trailing edge of the car
- Lift up windows

### Roadster S2 (*Type 26 S2 Roadster*)

![Type 26 Elan S2 with oval tail lights](faq/screenshots/identify/s2a.jpg)
![Type 26 Elan S2 alternate view](faq/screenshots/identify/s2b.jpg)

- No rollup windows
- Oval tail lights. Early cars had round lights
- Boot lid does not extend all the way to the trailing edge of the car
- Lift up windows

### Drophead S3 (*Type 45 S3 DHC*)

- Electric rollup windows with fixed frames
- Oval tail lights

### Drophead S3 S/E (*Type 45 S3 S/E DHC*)

![Type 45 S3 S/E DHC with electric rollup windows](faq/screenshots/identify/s3_se_dhc.jpg)

- Electric rollup windows with fixed frames
- Oval tail lights
- S/E badged
- Stainless steel side trims & wing mounted flasher repeaters on most cars

### Drophead S4 (*Type 45 S4 DHC*)

![Type 45 S4 DHC with square tail lights](faq/screenshots/identify/s4_dhc.jpg)

- Square tail lights
- Square profile wheelarches

### Drophead S4 S/E (*Type 45 S4 S/E DHC*)

![Type 45 S4 S/E DHC showing stainless steel trim](faq/screenshots/identify/s4_se_dhc_b.jpg)
![Type 45 S4 S/E DHC alternate view](faq/screenshots/identify/s4_se_dhc_a.jpg)

- Square tail lights
- Square profile wheelarches
- S/E badged
- Stainless steel side trims & wing mounted flasher repeaters on most cars

### Drophead Sprint (*Type 45 Sprint DHC*)

![Type 45 Sprint DHC with unique two-tone paint](faq/screenshots/identify/sprint_dhc.jpg)

- [Complete Sprint Details](http://www.lotuselansprint.com)
- Square tail lights
- Badged as Sprint
- Unique Two tone paint. Could be deleted as an option
- Square profile wheelarches

![Type 36 FHC pre-airflow showing no extractor grill](faq/screenshots/identify/s3_fhc_pre.jpg)

## Racing Version

### 26R (*Type 26 26R Race*)

![Type 26R race car with fixed headlights](faq/screenshots/identify/26r.jpg)

- Fixed headlights, Most not all
- Magnesium Lotus designed peg drive wheels
- S2 versions had lightweight bodies and flared fenders

## Plus 2

### Plus 2 (*Type 50 +2*)

![Type 50 Plus 2 dashboard showing 2 large and 4 small gauges](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2int.jpg)

- 50/0001 on
- 2 large gauges - Speedometer, Tachometer
- 4 small gauges - Water Temp, Oil Pressure, Ammeter, Fuel

### Plus 2 Federal (*Type 50 +2 Federal*)

- 50/0857 on for US, 50/0929 all markets
- 2 large gauges - Speedometer, Tachometer
- 4 small gauges - Water Temp, Oil Pressure, Ammeter, Fuel
- Remote boot release, flush interior door handles, modified exhaust

### Plus 2S (*Type 50 +2 2S*)

![Type 50 Plus 2S exterior view](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2sa.jpg)
![Type 50 Plus 2S alternate view](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2sb.jpg)

- 50/1593 on
- 2 large gauges - Speedometer, Tachometer
- 6 small gauges - Oil, Water temp, Battery Condition, Temp, Fuel, Clock
- 4 warning lights in center of dash (Hazard, Parking Brake, Brake Fail, Rear Screen)
- New luxury interior including revised seats and centre console
- Fog/Driving lamps below the bumper

### Plus 2S Federal (*Type 50 +2 2S Federal*)

- 50/2447 on
- 2 large gauges - Speedometer, Tachometer
- 6 small gauges - Oil, Water temp, Battery Condition, Temp, Fuel, Clock
- 4 warning lights in center of dash (Hazard, Parking Brake, Brake Fail, Rear Screen)
- Luxury interior including revised seats and centre console
- Fog/Driving lamps below the bumper

### Plus 2S 130 (*Type 50 +2 130*)

![Type 50 Plus 2S 130 exterior front](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2s130a.jpg)
![Type 50 Plus 2S 130 exterior rear](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2s130b.jpg)
![Type 50 Plus 2S 130 dashboard showing 6 small gauges and 3 warning lights](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2s130int.jpg)
![Type 50 Plus 2S 130 detail view](faq/screenshots/identify/plus2s130d.jpg)

- 71.01...0001 on
- 2 large gauges - Speedometer, Tachometer
- 6 small gauges - Oil, Water temp, Battery Condition, Temp, Fuel, Clock
- 3 warning lights in center of dash (Hazard, Park/Brake Fail, Rear Screen)
- Luxury interior including revised seats and centre console
- Big Valve engine
- Fog/Driving lamps below the bumper

### Plus 2S 130/5 (*Type 50 +2 130/5*)

- 72.10... on
- Same as Plus 2S 130 with 5-speed gearbox
