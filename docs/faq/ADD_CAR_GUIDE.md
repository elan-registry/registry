# How to Add Your Car to the Registry

Learn how to register your Lotus Elan or +2 in just 4 easy steps

This guide walks you through the complete process of adding your car
to the Lotus Elan Registry. The entire process takes about 5-10 minutes
and uses a simple step-by-step wizard.

## Before You Begin

### What You'll Need

- Your car's chassis number (see [Chassis Number
  Guide](#understanding-chassis-numbers) below)
- Basic information about your car (year, model, color)
- *Optional:* Engine number, purchase date, and history
- *Optional:* Photos of your car (up to 6 photos, max 3 MB each)

### Getting Started

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to "Add Car" from the main menu
3. You'll see a 4-step progress bar at the top showing:
   **Car Details → Additional Information → Images → Results**

## Step 1: Car Details

![Step 1 - Car Details](faq/screenshots/step1.png)

This is the most important step where you provide essential information
about your car.

### Required Fields (marked with *)

#### Year

- Select the model year from the dropdown (1963-1974)
- The dropdown shows all years when Lotus Elan and +2 models were
  produced
- ✅ A **green checkmark** appears when valid
- ❌ A **red X** appears if not selected

#### Model

- This dropdown becomes active after you select a year
- Shows only models available for the selected year
- Examples: "Elan 1600 (Type 26 S1 Roadster)", "Elan +2 (Type 50)"
- ✅ Green checkmark when valid
- ❌ Red X if not selected

#### Chassis Number

- Enter your car's chassis number *without* the type prefix
- **IMPORTANT:** Do not include the type (e.g., if your chassis is
  "26/0001", enter "0001")
- The system validates your chassis number format based on the year
- See [Understanding Chassis Numbers](#understanding-chassis-numbers)
  for detailed format requirements
- ✅ Green checkmark when valid and available
- ❌ Red X if invalid format or already registered

### Optional Fields

#### Color

- Enter the current exterior color of your car
- Example: "Yellow", "British Racing Green", "Red"

#### Engine Number

- Format: LPAxxxxx (where x is a digit)
- Example: "LPA12345"

### Understanding Chassis Numbers

The chassis number format changed over the years. The registry validates
your entry to ensure accuracy.

#### Quick Reference

- **Pre-1970 (1963-1969):** 4 digits only (e.g., `0001`)
- **1970 Transition:** 4 digits + letter OR 11 characters
- **Post-1970 (1971-1974):** 11 characters (YYMMBBssssT format)
- **Important:** Do NOT include the type prefix (26/, 36/, etc.)

#### For Complete Format Details

See the **[Chassis Validation Rules](../chassis-validation.php)** page
for comprehensive information including:

- Detailed format specifications for each period
- Valid type letters by model
- Special cases and exceptions
- Visual examples and validation logic

### Chassis Validation Help

If your chassis number doesn't validate:

1. **Click "Chassis Validation Rules"** - Opens a quick reference guide
2. **Review the format** for your model year
3. **Check for common errors:**
   - Including the type prefix (26/, 36/, etc.)
   - Wrong number of digits
   - Invalid type letter
   - Letter "I" instead of "1"

### Override Option

If you have historical documentation supporting a chassis number that
doesn't validate:

1. Check the **"⚠️ Override chassis validation"** box
2. Use this option with caution
3. You can proceed to the next step even with validation errors
4. Make sure you've verified the chassis number is correct

### Continue to Step 2

Once all required fields show **green checkmarks**, click the
**"Next"** button to proceed to Step 2.

## Step 2: Additional Information

![Step 2 - Additional Information](faq/screenshots/step2.png)

Add optional details about your car's history and provide a way for
other enthusiasts to learn more about your car.

### Optional Fields (All)

#### Purchase Date

- Click the calendar icon to open the date picker
- Format: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2025-09-15)
- Enter the approximate date you acquired the car
- Leave blank if unknown

#### Sold Date

- Only fill this if you no longer own the car
- Format: YYYY-MM-DD
- Helps track ownership history

#### Website

- Personal website, blog, or social media page about your car
- Format: Full URL (e.g., `<https://myelan.com>`)
- Visitors can click to learn more about your car

#### Comments

- Large text area for your car's story
- Share your car's history, restoration details, or special features
- Examples:
  - "Original owner since 1972. Numbers-matching, underwent complete
    restoration in 2010."
  - "Barn find from California. Currently undergoing sympathetic
    restoration."
  - "Restored to original Lotus Yellow with period-correct S4 upgrades."

### Tips for Comments

- **Be specific:** Include restoration history, modifications, or
  notable features
- **Mention provenance:** Previous owners, race history, or interesting
  backstory
- **Note condition:** Original, restored, race-prepared, etc.
- **Add personality:** This is your chance to share what makes your car
  special

### Step 2 Navigation

- **Previous** - Return to Step 1 to make changes
- **Next** - Proceed to Step 3 (Photo Upload)

## Step 3: Upload Photos

![Step 3 - Image Upload](faq/screenshots/step3.png)

Showcase your car with photos! Images help other registry members
appreciate your car and increase engagement.

### Photo Requirements

- **Maximum:** 6 photos
- **File size:** Up to 3.00 MB each
- **Format:** JPEG, PNG, or other image formats
- **Resolution:** Photos are automatically resized for optimal web
  display

### How to Upload Photos

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop

1. Open your file browser/Finder
2. Select one or more photos
3. Drag them into the upload area
4. Photos appear as thumbnails immediately

#### Method 2: Click to Browse

1. Click anywhere in the upload area
2. Browse and select photos from your computer
3. Click "Open" to upload

### Managing Your Photos

#### Reorder Photos

- Click and drag thumbnails to reorder
- The first photo becomes your car's primary image

#### Remove Photos

- Click "Remove photo" link below any thumbnail
- Photo is removed immediately

### Photo Tips

#### Best practices for great car photos

- **Full car shots:** Front 3/4 view, side profile, rear 3/4 view
- **Detail shots:** Engine bay, interior, special features
- **Good lighting:** Natural daylight shows colors accurately
- **Clean background:** Park on grass or plain background when possible
- **Multiple angles:** Show your car's best features

#### Common mistakes to avoid

- Blurry or out-of-focus images
- Very dark or overexposed photos
- Photos with clutter in the background
- Extremely large file sizes (over 3 MB)

### Proceeding Without Photos

You can skip photo upload and add photos later:

1. Leave the upload area empty
2. Click "Add Car" to complete registration
3. Return later to edit your car and add photos

### Step 3 Navigation

- **Previous** - Return to Step 2 to modify information
- **Add Car** - Submit your car to the registry!

## Step 4: Review and Confirm

![Step 4 - Results](faq/screenshots/step4.png)

After clicking "Add Car", your submission is processed and you'll see
your completed car profile.

### What Happens Next

#### Immediate

- Your car is added to the registry database
- A unique Registry ID is assigned
- Your car appears in registry searches
- Other members can view your car's details

#### Your Car's Profile Includes

- **Vehicle Information:** Year, model, series, type, chassis
- **Appearance & Technical:** Color, engine number
- **Ownership & History:** Purchase date, comments
- **Photos:** Image gallery (if uploaded)
- **Owner Information:** Your name and location
- **Location Map:** Approximate location on map
- **Update History:** Timestamp of when car was added

### Managing Your Car

From your car's detail page, you can:

- **Update Car** - Edit any information or add/remove photos
- **View Website** - Link to your car's website (if provided)
- **Track Updates** - See history of all changes made

### Sharing Your Car

- Your car is now publicly viewable in the registry
- Share the direct link with friends and fellow enthusiasts
- Your car appears in filtered searches by year, model, or location

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Problem: "Chassis Validation Failed" Error

**Solutions:**

1. Remove any type prefix (26/, 36/, etc.) - enter numbers only
2. Check the format guide for your model year
3. Verify you haven't confused letter "I" with number "1"
4. Use the "Override chassis validation" checkbox if you have
   documentation

#### Problem: "This chassis number is already registered" Warning

**Solution:** See [Chassis Already Registered?](#chassis-already-registered)
below

#### Problem: "Model dropdown is disabled"

**Solution:** Select a year first - the model dropdown activates
automatically

#### Problem: "Cannot proceed to next step"

**Solutions:**

- Look for **red X** icons - these fields need correction
- All required fields must show **green checkmarks**

#### Problem: "Photo upload failed" or "File too large"

**Solutions:**

1. Resize photo to smaller dimensions (1920x1080 is plenty)
2. Compress photo using image editing software
3. Convert to JPEG format (usually smaller than PNG)
4. Try uploading one photo at a time

#### Problem: "Cannot find my car's model in the dropdown"

**Solutions:**

1. Choose the closest matching model
2. Specify exact variant in the Comments field
3. Contact the registry administrator for assistance

## Chassis Already Registered?

If you try to enter a chassis number that's already in the registry,
you'll see a **yellow warning box** with information about requesting
a transfer.

### What This Means

- The chassis number is already registered to another member
- This protects against duplicate entries
- The current owner may no longer own the car

### Request Ownership Transfer

If you're the new owner of a car already in the registry:

1. **Review the warning message** - Confirms chassis is taken
2. **Click "Request Ownership Transfer"** button
3. **Confirm your request** in the popup dialog
4. **Notification sent** to:
   - Current registered owner (via email)
   - Registry administrators
5. **Wait for response** - You'll receive email when processed

### Transfer Process

1. Current owner receives your transfer request
2. They can approve or deny the transfer
3. Administrators can assist if owner doesn't respond
4. Once approved, ownership transfers to your account
5. You can then update all car information
